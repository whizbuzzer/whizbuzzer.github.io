About Me

I am from Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

शिवाजीराज्यातुन मी, स्वागत कर माझा.

I have studied Robotics, Control systems design, Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, DNNs and Machine Learning, but my focus is Computer Vision.
