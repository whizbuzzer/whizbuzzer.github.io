About Me

I hail from Mumbai, the largest and undisputedly the best city of India.

I have graduated with a BE in Electronics and Telecommunication from Shah and Anchor Kutchhi Engineering College (SAKEC). I have graduated with an MS in Robotics and Autonomous Systems from Arizona State University (ASU).

I aspire to be a consummate roboticist with a life full of deeds and a heart full of purpose. I'm interested in Robotics, Control systems design, Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, DNNs and Machine Learning although I am more focused on CV and DNNs. I also aspire to be Maharashtrian so fantastic that Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj himself would be proud of me.

I know 6 languages; Marathi, English, Hindi, Russian, Japanese and Spanish. I love sketching, painting, calligraphy, dancing, singing and playing the piano.
