Deeds

कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन।
मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि॥ २-४७

You have the right to act, but not to the fruits of your actions.
Neither should be the fruits of your actions be your aim, nor should you (have) attachment to inaction.

tl;dr: Bear no expectations and do things just because you want to so that in the sad case where you don't get what you wanted, you keep going on.
even tl;dr: Just keep doing things and don't stop.

― Lord Krishna, Bhagavad Gita 2:47
